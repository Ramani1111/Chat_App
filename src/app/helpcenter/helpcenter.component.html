Troubleshooting

  • Can't log in

    If you're having trouble logging in:

    1. Ensure you're using the correct phone number.
    2. Check your internet connection.
    3. If the issue persists, contact support.

  • Messages not sending

    If your messages aren't sending:

    1. Check your internet connection.
    2. Ensure the recipient's phone number is correct.
    3. Restart the app or your device.

  • App crashes

    If the app keeps crashing:

    1. Update the app to the latest version.
    2. Clear the app cache and data.
    3. Reinstall the app if the issue persists.
